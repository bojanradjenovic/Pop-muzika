Charli xcx


Charli xcx, whose real name is Charlotte Emma Aitchison, is a British singer, songwriter, and producer, born on August 2, 1992, in Cambridge. Known for her innovative approach to pop music, Charli started her career by releasing songs on MySpace, which attracted public attention and interest from record labels.

Her popularity grew thanks to hits like Boom Clap (2014), Fancy (in collaboration with Iggy Azalea), and I Love It (which she wrote for Icona Pop). Albums like Charli (2019), Crash (2022), and BRAT (2024) solidified her status as one of the most innovative figures in modern pop music. She often experiments with hyperpop and electronic sounds, while her songs explore themes of love, freedom, and individuality.

Charli xcx is known for her futuristic approach to music and style, as well as collaborations with numerous artists such as Troye Sivan, SOPHIE, and Christine and the Queens. Her concerts are energetic and full of experimental elements, bringing a refreshing vibe to the pop scene.

I admire her bravery in constantly pushing the boundaries of the genre. The album BRAT is one of my most-listened-to albums in 2024.
